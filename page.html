Congratulations! You are the new Site-28 Containment Director.

You are responsible for the recovery and initial containment of newly-discovered anomalies within the New York area.

Automated surveillance systems will provide you with news stories, police reports, or other events flagged for possible anomalous influence, and you will need to triage and investigate them.

You must balance the needs of efficient containment, preservation of the veil, and consumption of resources, all while minimizing casualties.

We've received a few potential anomaly reports (PARs). These may be anomalous objects, people, or events, but occasionally are false positives.

You will investigate one at a time, and must either dismiss the report or request an SCP designation in order to mark it as complete.

Your supervisor, Site Director Sandoval, will receive automated reports on your progress in this role.
